“[Love’s] flames are a blazing fire, the flame of Jah.” - SONG OF SOL. 8:6.

We, Taehong and Nahyun, who try to pleasure Jehovah’s heart fully,
decided to get married on Sept. 7th at 5 pm.

It saddens us that we can't be with our beloved friends on our special day.
 But we would like to take this opportunity to thank you for supporting us and  for showing us your love.

  We are always grateful to Jehovah for giving you all to us.

  Thank you for blessing us. We will try to become a couple guided by Jehovah's love. Living wisely and building up our faith together.
